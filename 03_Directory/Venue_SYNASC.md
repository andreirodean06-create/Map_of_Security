# Venue: SYNASC

* **Context:** International Symposium on Symbolic and Numeric Algorithms for Scientific Computing, hosted locally by the Department of Computer Science at UVT.
* **Security importance:** Security often depends on correct algorithms, verification, formal logic, reliable software, and efficient computation. These topics can connect to cryptography, static analysis, testing, formal methods, and secure systems.
* **Connected to:** [[UVT_CS_Security_Landscape]], [[Cryptography_Map]], [[Application_Software_Security_Map]], [[Systems_Infrastructure_Security_Map]]
* **Student action:** https://synasc.ro/2026/. Look for talks about formal methods, verification, cryptography, distributed systems, software engineering, or AI reliability.
