# Dumitru Penteliuc

* **Institution:** [[UVT_CS_Security_Landscape]]
* **Contact Email:** dumitru.penteliuc@e-uvt.ro
* **Domain Alignment:** [[Symmetric_Crypto_Hashing]], [[Public_Key_Crypto_PKI]]
* **Core Focus:** Foundations of Cryptography, Symmetric Block Ciphers, Discrete Mathematics, and Applied Information Security.

## Academic & Security Relevance
Dumitru Penteliuc coordinates the direct, hands-on instructional laboratory modules covering core cryptography courses at the Department of Informatics. He is responsible for guiding students through the concrete software construction, mathematical constraints, and execution requirements of data encryption standards, block cipher modes (AES/DES), initialization vectors, message authentication codes (MACs), and cryptographic collision resistance validations.
