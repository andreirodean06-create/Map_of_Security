## Important Problems

* **Attribution uncertainty:** different attackers reuse tools and infrastructure.
* **Noisy indicators:** IPs and domains change quickly.
* **Non-actionable reports:** interesting information does not lead to a decision.
* **Open problem:** turning broad intelligence into local defensive priorities.
