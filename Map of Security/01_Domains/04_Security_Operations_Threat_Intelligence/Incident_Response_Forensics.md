## Important Problems

* **Unclear ownership:** nobody knows who decides containment.
* **Evidence loss:** logs rotate or systems are wiped before analysis.
* **Recovery without lessons:** the same incident happens again.
* **Open problem:** fast response while preserving enough evidence for learning and accountability.
