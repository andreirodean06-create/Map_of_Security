## Important Problems

* **Data overcollection:** systems store data they do not need.
* **Sensitive logs:** personal data appears in logs or analytics.
* **Weak deletion:** "delete account" does not actually remove or anonymize data.
* **Open problem:** making privacy usable while keeping systems functional and auditable.
