## Important Problems

* **Everything is high risk:** risk ratings become useless if nothing is prioritized.
* **Audit-only security:** passing a checklist does not mean the system is secure.
* **Unclear asset ownership:** nobody knows who accepts or fixes a risk.
* **Open problem:** translating technical findings into decisions leaders can act on.
