## Important Problems

* **Phishing:** attackers trick people into giving credentials or running malware.
* **Security fatigue:** users ignore warnings they see too often.
* **Bad recovery flows:** attackers abuse password reset or support processes.
* **Open problem:** designing controls that people can realistically follow.
